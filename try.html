Identifying Your Conflict Management Styles

Read each statement carefully. Use the scale: 1-Never, 2-Seldom, 3-Sometimes, 4-Frequently, 5-Very typical

1. It is easier to refrain than to retreat from a quarrel.
忍受爭吵總比在爭吵中退縮容易得多。

2. If you cannot make a person think as you do, make him do as you think.
假如你不能使一個人和你的想法一樣，就著令他按你的想法去做事。

3. Soft words win hard hearts
為人溫婉比鐵石心腸更優勝。

4. You scratch my back, I’ll scratch yours.
你敬我一尺，我敬你一丈。

5. Come now and let us reason together.
來吧！讓我們理性地一同處理問題。

6. When two quarrel, the person who keeps silent first is the most praise-worthy.
當兩個人爭吵，先安靜的一方是最值得稱讚。

7. Might overcomes right.
能力 / 力量可勝權利。

8. When one hits you with a stone, hit him a piece of cotton.
當人家向你擲石頭，你最多向他擲一片棉花，以德報怨。

9. Better half a loaf than no bread at all.
有半杯水總比無水的好，聊勝於無。

10. Truth lies in knowledge, not in majority opinion.
知識包含真理，但大眾未必有此想法。

11. There is nothing so important you have to fight for it.
沒有任何重要的事值得我去爭取。

12. There are two kinds of people in the world, the winners and the losers.
世上只有兩種人：成功者和失敗者。

13. Kill you enemies with kindness.
用仁慈的方法殺掉你的敵人。

14. A fair exchange brings no quarrel.
公平的交易不會帶來爭吵。

15. No person has the final answer but every person has a piece to contribute.
雖然可能沒有人找到最後的答案，但是每個人都有一定的貢獻。

16. Stay away from people who disagree with you.
盡量避免與你持不同意見的人接觸。

17. Battles are won by those who believe in winning.
在鬥爭中，想勝利的人必會勝出。

18. Kind words are worth much and cost little.
仁慈的字句是有價值的，而且付出的成本很低。

19. When both people give in halfway, a fair settlement is achieved.
當兩個人均作出適當的讓步，共識便能達成。

20. By digging and digging, the truth is discovered.
「發掘再發掘」真理是會出現的。

21. Avoid quarrelsome people as they will only make your life miserable.
避開一些喜歡爭吵的人，因為他們會令你生活造成痛苦。

22. A person who will not flee will make others flee.
若一個人堅持不肯退出，其他人就會因此而退出或作出讓步。

23. Soft words ensure harmony.
言詞溫婉能保證和諧。

24. One gift for another makes good friends.
送禮物予人家，人家就會成為你的朋友。

25. Bring your conflicts into the open and face them directly; only then will the best solution be discovered.
你會將衝突公開和面對面處理，因為只有這樣才有最佳的解決方法。

26. The best way of handling conflicts is to avoid them.
處理衝突的最佳方法便是逃避。

27. Put your foot down where you mean to stand.
當你有了明確的立場，才會表態。

28. Gentleness will triumph over anger.
和善是有利於憤怒。

29. Getting part of what you want is better than to getting anything at all.
在你想要的東西中得到一些，總比甚麼也得不到的好。

30. Frankness, honesty and trust will move mountains.
憑著坦率、誠實和信任，沒有事情是不能解決的。
